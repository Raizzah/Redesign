About Us


It is our goal to exceed your expectations. Come experience a family atmosphere where you come as guests and leave as friends. Our staff is highly trained with diversity in all aspects of haircuts, hair coloring, and hair treatments. We keep up on the latest trends in our industry by continually attending hair shows and education classes throughout the year. Established in 2011, Inspire Hair salon has been featured on the Bay Area A-List for best haircuts 3 years in a row. Come let us pamper you and provide you with quality services.
