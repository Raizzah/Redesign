Salon Policy

All service(s) are guaranteed during the time of your appointment. There will be no refunds or re- do's for any service(s) following the date of your appointment time. If there are any issues with your service, they must be corrected during your accommodated time frame of your appointment, no exceptions will be made.

Cancellation

Please call the salon to cancel or reschedule your appointment within 24 hours of your appointment if you cannot make it. If you are late by 10 minutes your appointment will automatically be cancelled. If you no call, no show, or cancel on the same day, your appointment will automatically be cancelled and you will be placed on a walk in only basis.
